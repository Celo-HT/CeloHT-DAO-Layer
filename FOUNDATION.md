# CeloHT Foundation 

CeloHT is built on three core pillars:

## 1. Education
- Local training programs  
- Educational materials  
- Scholarships and learning support  

## 2. Agents
- Network of local agents for onboarding through Valora  
- User support and community assistance  
- Field representation for the ecosystem  

## 3. Reforestation
- ESG-focused environmental projects  
- Tree planting initiatives  
- Transparent tracking and reporting of funding and impact  


## Structure

### DAO (Community)
- Holds final decision-making power  
- Approves major proposals, funding, and strategic direction  

### Foundation Stewards
- Operational team  
- Executes programs, proposes initiatives, strengthens long-term strategy  

### Founder / CEO
- Provides vision and external representation  
- No centralized control or unilateral authority
